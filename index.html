About Me

Hello there! I'm Joshua Visagie, a 28-year-old entrepreneur with a passion for language education, creativity, and technology. I currently run an online English school while also indulging my artistic side through graphic design and programming projects. The dynamic combination of language and technology fuels my professional pursuits.


Beyond the digital realm, I find joy in the world of art and devote time to fostering community connections. I actively contribute to my local game store by organizing and hosting board game evenings, creating a welcoming space for enthusiasts to come together.


My journey took an exciting turn when I lived in China for approximately five years. The experience was more than a geographic shift; it was a transformative period that broadened my perspectives about the world and deepened my understanding of myself. Travel, for me, is not just a physical journey but a profound exploration that continually shapes my worldview.


As an individual, I pride myself on my independence, yet I value the synergy of teamwork. I believe that collaboration enhances creativity and brings out the best in each team member. This website serves as a window into my world, and I hope you discover more about my ventures, experiences, and the lessons I've gathered along the way.
